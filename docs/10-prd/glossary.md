# Glossary

## Partner

Canonical meaning: a participant who joins or acts inside a `PartnerRequest`.

Chinese term: 搭子.

Notes:

- A Partner exists only relative to a `PartnerRequest`.
- Joining a partner request is what makes a user a Partner in that request.

## PartnerRequest

Canonical meaning: a request that defines expectations, roles, and participation context for partners.

Chinese term: 搭子请求.

Notes:

- This is the core unit users browse, create, apply to, and discuss.

## PartnerRole

Canonical meaning: a role a Partner may apply for or hold inside a `PartnerRequest`.

Chinese term: 搭子角色.

Notes:

- Roles define rights, obligations, and expected participation.

## PartnerApplication

Canonical meaning: a user's request to join a `PartnerRequest`.

Chinese term: 搭子申请.

Notes:

- A non-partner user becomes eligible to join by submitting a PartnerApplication.

## PartnerSubApplication

Canonical meaning: an individual role application inside a `PartnerApplication`.

Notes:

- Each sub-application corresponds to one intended `PartnerRole` and an application reason.
- Approval of any sub-application approves the whole PartnerApplication.

## Chat

Canonical meaning: a messaging channel between partners or partner-related participants.

## Notification

Canonical meaning: a surfaced event about partner request, application, or chat activity.

## Explore

Canonical meaning: the product surface where users browse partner requests.

## Profile

Canonical meaning: the user account surface for identity and personal details.
