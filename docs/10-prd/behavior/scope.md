# Scope

## In scope

- Partner request creation, browsing, detail viewing, and application flows.
- Role-based participation inside partner requests.
- Chat and notification surfaces for partner-related activity.
- Account and profile management.
- Multi-locale UI copy.

## Out of scope

- A fully specified matching or ranking algorithm.
- Full moderation, safety, or trust policy.
- Payment, billing, or settlement workflows.
- Complete social networking beyond partner coordination.

## Open questions

- What are the matching and ranking rules for partner requests?
- What moderation or safety checks are required for partner participation?
- Are payment, billing, verification, or student-auth workflows required?
- What offline, archival, or expiry behaviors are expected for old requests and chats?
- What privacy rules govern visibility of profiles, applications, and partner requests?
