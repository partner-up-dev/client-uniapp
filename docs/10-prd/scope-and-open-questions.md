# Scope and open questions

## In scope

- Partner request creation, browsing, and application flows
- Role-based participation inside partner requests
- Chat and notification surfaces
- Account and profile management
- Multi-locale UI copy

## Open questions

- What are the matching and ranking rules for partner requests
- What moderation or safety checks are required for partner participation
- Are there payment, billing, or verification workflows
- What offline or archival behaviors are expected for old requests and chats
- What privacy rules govern visibility of partner profiles and requests
