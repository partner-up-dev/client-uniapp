# Hard constraints

## Mini-program platforms

Constraint:

- The client targets mini-program platforms, primarily Weixin and Alipay.

Product impact:

- Product behavior must fit compact mobile interaction patterns and platform capability limits.

## Structured creation flow

Constraint:

- Partner requests are created through structured steps rather than free-form posting only.

Product impact:

- Request data must be explicit enough to support browsing, role selection, and application decisions.

## Localization requirement

Constraint:

- User-facing copy should be localized.

Product impact:

- Product surfaces should avoid hard-coded user-facing text and use stable copy keys.

## Authentication dependency

Constraint:

- App launch attempts account login and product flows depend on account state.

Product impact:

- Profile, application, chat, and notification behavior should account for session availability.
