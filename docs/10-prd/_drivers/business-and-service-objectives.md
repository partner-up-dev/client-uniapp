# Business and service objectives

## Partner request marketplace

Objective:

- Let users create, browse, and join structured partner requests.

Success rationale:

- PartnerUp's core value depends on turning loose social intent into actionable requests with clear participation boundaries.

## Role-based participation

Objective:

- Support different roles inside one partner request.

Success rationale:

- Roles make expectations explicit and reduce mismatch between request creators and applicants.

## Account and profile continuity

Objective:

- Let users maintain profile information that can be reused across partner flows.

Success rationale:

- Partner trust and coordination depend on stable identity and profile context.

## Activity awareness

Objective:

- Surface chat and notification activity relevant to partner requests.

Success rationale:

- Partner flows need follow-up coordination after browsing, applying, or joining.

## Platform reach

Objective:

- Keep the client viable on Weixin and Alipay mini-program platforms.

Success rationale:

- Mini-program availability is a core distribution and usage constraint for the product.
