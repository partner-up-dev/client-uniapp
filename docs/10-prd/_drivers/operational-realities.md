# Operational realities

## Current client surfaces are partial

Reality:

- The current client has pages and components for browsing, creating, applying, chat, notifications, and profile management, but some backend-integrated flows are still incomplete or open.

Product impact:

- Claims in this PRD describe intended product behavior inferred from current surfaces and should be refined as backend and policy decisions mature.

## Matching and ranking are unsettled

Reality:

- Partner request matching and ranking rules are not yet defined in durable product truth.

Product impact:

- Browse and explore claims should avoid promising a specific ranking strategy until confirmed.

## Safety and moderation are unsettled

Reality:

- Moderation, safety checks, student verification, and privacy rules are open questions.

Product impact:

- Participation claims should not imply trust guarantees that are not yet specified.

## Historical docs were mixed

Reality:

- Product semantics previously lived partly in flat PRD files and partly in topic notes.

Product impact:

- This PRD consolidates product semantics into one owner to prevent future drift.
